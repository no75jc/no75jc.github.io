Established in Secrecy

No. 75 draws inspiration from the early 1900s—an era of craftsmanship, restraint, and quiet confidence.

Once a financial institution, the building’s history informs a space designed for discretion: subdued lighting, rich materials, and cocktails prepared with intention rather than excess.

Entry is subtle. The experience is deliberate. Nothing here asks for attention—yet everything rewards it.

Atmosphere

Candlelight, dark wood, and quiet corners inspired by private rooms and bank vaults of the early twentieth century.

Cocktails

Spirit-forward classics and original compositions built with precision, balance, and restraint.

Capacity

Intentionally limited seating to preserve intimacy and attention to detail.
